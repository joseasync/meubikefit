Meu Bikefit

Video-based bike fitting by Partícula Unânime – Ride smarter, safer, and more efficiently

Meu Bikefit is an innovative project by Partícula Unânime that uses advanced video analysis to help cyclists achieve optimal riding posture. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, our remote bike fitting system delivers expert adjustments tailored to your body – all through video.

Benefits of a Proper Bike Fit

  • Maximized power and pedal efficiency
  • Enhanced comfort for long-distance rides
  • Reduced injury risk and pain prevention
  • Optimized posture and alignment
  • Performance tuning based on body geometry

How It Works

  • Record a side-view video of yourself riding (indoors or outdoors)
  • Upload your video to our platform
  • We analyze your position using AI + cycling expertise
  • Receive a personalized fit report and visual suggestions
